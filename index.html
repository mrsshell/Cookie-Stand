Our Story

Pat’ Famous Salmon Cookies secret recipe is revolutionizing the cookie industry. First discovered in 1990 during a fishing expedition into the Yukon where fish are so plentiful a man can cross a river walking on their backs.

To give the team a treat he came up with the formula to make salmon cookies sweet and delicious. They became a big hit with both the team and the wildlife.

When Pat moved to Seattle, he adopted many cats who share their love for these delightful treets. Today Pat’s Famous Salmon cookies have become a household staple, and tourist attraction as well.

Pat’s Famous Salmon Cookies with a glass of milk will make cats and children alike say, "meow."
